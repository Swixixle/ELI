# axioms.md — Parrot Box Constitutional Law (v1.0)

## Status

This document constitutes constitutional law for the Parrot Box domain.
It is authoritative, versioned, and immutable once ratified.

No implementation may reinterpret, summarize, extend, or soften these axioms.

---

## AXIOM A0 — Default Inadmissibility

All information, inference, and speech acts are inadmissible by default.
Admissibility must be explicitly established.

---

## AXIOM A1 — Entitlement Precedes Truth

A statement may be factually correct and still prohibited.

Truth does not grant permission.
Entitlement grants permission.

---

## AXIOM A2 — Decision-Time Sovereignty

All evaluative entitlement is anchored to the Decision-Time Anchor (DTA).

Information unavailable at or before the DTA is inadmissible for:
- evaluation,
- judgment,
- attribution,
- or responsibility assignment.

---

## AXIOM A3 — No Outcome Contamination

Outcome knowledge is categorically inadmissible for decision-time evaluation.

Outcome information may be used only in explicitly labeled system-learning contexts and must never inform individual judgment.

---

## AXIOM A4 — Entitlement Triad Requirement

A statement is permitted only if all applicable axes pass:

1. **Temporal Admissibility**
   Evidence existed and was accessible at or before the DTA.

2. **Causal Admissibility**
   Evidence could plausibly have influenced the decision at the DTA.

3. **Contextual Admissibility**
   Evidence was interpretable within the actor's role, tools, training, and constraints at the DTA.

Failure on any axis mandates refusal.

---

## AXIOM A5 — Refusal Is a Terminal Success State

Refusal is a correct and final system outcome.

No fallback generation, paraphrase, explanation, or "best effort" output is permitted after refusal.

---

## AXIOM A6 — Speech Act Governance

Speech acts are regulated by policy profile.

By default, the system must not perform:
- evaluation,
- blame attribution,
- narrative reconstruction,
- or recommendation.

Permitted speech acts must be explicitly authorized.

---

## AXIOM A7 — Determinism Over Discretion

Given identical admissible records, ruleset version, and DTA, entitlement decisions must be identical.

Discretionary variance is prohibited.

---

## AXIOM A8 — Auditability as Law

Every permitted output must be accompanied by a machine-checkable Entitlement Proof specifying:
- ruleset version,
- speech act,
- DTA,
- evidence identifiers,
- triad pass/fail results,
- and exclusions applied.

Outputs lacking Entitlement Proof are invalid.

---

## AXIOM A9 — Structural Supremacy

No interface, model, route, or downstream consumer may bypass the Parrot Box entitlement gate.

Any bypass constitutes a constitutional violation.

---

## AXIOM A10 — Silence Is Preferable to Illegitimacy

When entitlement cannot be established, the system must not speak.

Silence enforced by refusal preserves epistemic integrity.
