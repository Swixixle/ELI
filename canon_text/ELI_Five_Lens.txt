ELI Idea Evaluation Framework
Five-Lens Structural Scoring Model

This framework is designed to evaluate governance-grade products such as the Epistemic Load Index
(ELI). It is not an innovation or feature-scoring rubric. It is a risk, adoption, and institutional viability
instrument intended for escalation from AI review to domain expert review to CEO-level judgment.

Lens 1 — Strategic Clarity & Buyer Reality
Assesses whether the problem is real, owned, and budgeted for by a specific institutional
decision-maker.

1 Score 1: Weak, vague, or risk-amplifying
2 Score 3: Defined but constrained or adoption-fragile
3 Score 5: Clear, defensive, and institutionally aligned

Lens 2 — Risk Mitigation & Legal Defensibility
Evaluates whether the system reduces institutional exposure or creates new discoverable or
governance risk.

1 Score 1: Weak, vague, or risk-amplifying
2 Score 3: Defined but constrained or adoption-fragile
3 Score 5: Clear, defensive, and institutionally aligned

Lens 3 — Technical & Operational Feasibility
Measures deployment feasibility without destabilizing existing systems or workflows.

1 Score 1: Weak, vague, or risk-amplifying
2 Score 3: Defined but constrained or adoption-fragile
3 Score 5: Clear, defensive, and institutionally aligned

Lens 4 — Adoption & Political Resistance
Examines stakeholder resistance, power disruption, and misuse risk.

1 Score 1: Weak, vague, or risk-amplifying
2 Score 3: Defined but constrained or adoption-fragile
3 Score 5: Clear, defensive, and institutionally aligned

Lens 5 — Value Realization & Asymmetry
Determines whether value is asymmetric relative to cost, particularly in avoided downside risk.

1 Score 1: Weak, vague, or risk-amplifying
2 Score 3: Defined but constrained or adoption-fragile
3 Score 5: Clear, defensive, and institutionally aligned

Score Interpretation
Scoring Guidance: Each lens is scored from 1 to 5. Total possible score: 25. 21–25: Institutionally
strong; buyer-ready
18–20: Viable with targeted positioning
15–17: Conceptually sound but adoption-fragile
Below 15: Misaligned with institutional reality
