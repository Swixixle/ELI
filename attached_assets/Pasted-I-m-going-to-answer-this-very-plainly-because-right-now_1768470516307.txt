2️⃣ What is the USER supposed to do next?

Right now, the system says:

“Here are gaps.”

So let’s define that.

⸻

The missing concept: Decision Phase

Every case must clearly be in ONE phase.

Here is the only set that makes sense:

Phase 1 — Intake

Goal: Get the case into a reviewable state
Success looks like: “Preliminary review allowed”

Phase 2 — Review

Goal: Determine whether a conclusion is permissible
Success looks like: “Decision allowed” or “Decision blocked”

Phase 3 — Decision

Goal: Record a defensible determination
Success looks like: “Decision logged + frozen”

Phase 4 — Closure

Goal: Preserve record for audit
Success looks like: “Case closed / archived”

Right now, your UI never tells the user:
	•	Which phase they are in
	•	What phase comes next
	•	What they are trying to unlock

So everything feels endless.

⸻

3️⃣ What does SUCCESS look like in this system?

This is the most important part.

❌ Success is NOT:
	•	“No gaps identified”
	•	“Perfect documentation”
	•	“Total certainty”

That’s an impossible and dangerous standard.

✅ Success IS:
	•	The system explicitly says:
“A decision is now procedurally permissible.”

or
	•	The system explicitly says:
“A decision is procedurally blocked, and here is why.”

That’s the win condition.

Anything else is noise.
